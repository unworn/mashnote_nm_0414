mashnote

A way to anonymously send positive feedback. MORE

Mashnote grew out of the realization that we rarely celebrate the people in our lives, and all the details that make them great, until it's too late. We created Mashnote so you could say the specific things you admire about your close friend, your sister, the colleague on your team at work, the guy at the deli -- and so you could do so anonymously.

Mashnote is a twist on the secret admirer notes of simpler times. You send an anonymous telegram that's affirmative, positive, and encouraging. Now is your chance -- don't let life pass without doing it.
